Concurrently, advanced research is pushing toward automated safety experimentation and decentralized alignment frameworks to govern increasingly autonomous reasoning agents.

9. **[KISS AI Safety](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/JsTRjtqbwN5EtNEiC/kiss-ai-safety)** — neutral
